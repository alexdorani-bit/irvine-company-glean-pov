Why Glean is the right platform

Built for this — not bolted on

Glean is a horizontal Work AI layer — not a point solution. TIC gets a single platform across every use case, every team, and every building.

Connected Context

One layer across every system

Native connectors to Salesforce, ServiceNow, SharePoint, Google Workspace, and 120+ more. Glean builds an enterprise knowledge graph across people, projects, and systems — pulling full context instead of forcing app-by-app searches.

Governed by Default

Permission-aware — always

Glean inherits source app permissions and never broadens access. Sensitive lease data, legal documents, and financial records stay governed. Critical for a company managing billions in long-term real estate assets.

Flexible AI

LLM-agnostic — including Gemini

TIC's GCP and Gemini strategy is preserved. Glean is model-agnostic — departments can select OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini for any query or agent task. No vendor lock-in at the AI layer.

Agents at Scale

Glean's agent platform lets property managers, leasing reps, and ops leads build and deploy agents — no engineering required. Codify the highest-value workflows and make them reusable across the portfolio.
